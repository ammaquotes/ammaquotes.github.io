Things Amma Actually Said!

Amma Quotes

An incomplete list of ridiculous things that Amma has actually said.

Inspired by Ms. B.

January 27th, 2019

Amma: "Are you making good grades?! Grades are important for getting into graduate school.”

Me: "Me: Yes, but... you act like my life has some predetermined linear trajectory: high school to college to graduate school to professor..."

Amma: "Yes! That's correct. And along that linear trajectory, you need to marry an Indian girl."

January 27th, 2019

Me: *picks up the phone and says "hello" quietly*

Amma: "Where are you?"

Me: "The library."

Amma: "Why are you always in the library... Kanna, do you have enough friends??"

January 12th, 2019

Amma: “No alcohol! Alcohol makes you lose weight!!”

January 12th, 2019

*first call home this year*

Appa: “Can you start doing something right now to improve your chances of getting into law school?”

Me: “I just started my second term of college…”

Amma: “Yeah kanna, don’t worry about law school. You need to focus on your health. Please eat more.”

Me: *laughing*

Appa: “It’s not funny! People start working on their law school applications at age 5. They clear the bar at 6th grade!”

January 12th, 2019

Amma: “All we want for you is to be healthy and alive.”

Appa: “You will become nothing. You know the story of Ramanujan?”

Me: “I know who Ramanujan is, but he died because of Tuberculosis, not eating too little.”

Appa: “Yes, but his immunity went down when he went to England because he didn’t eat enough.”

December 29th, 2018

Amma to Anand, in front of my face: "Anand, you know kanna, of all the children, you have the most promise."

December 27th, 2018

Amma: Who did you buy that Michelle Obama book for?

Me: Myself.

Amma: YOU want to become Michelle Obama??

November 21st, 2018

The literal first words Amma says as I walk in the door: "Kanna, you lost some weight?!?!"

October 13th, 2018

Amma: "You need to get a haircut."

Me: "Actually, I think I'm going to grow my hair out."

Appa: "We don't need another Karthik in this house."

October 7th, 2018

Amma over the phone: "In general, do you do any exercise??"

September 2nd, 2018

*Over Facetime*

Amma: "You don't want to over-gain weight... Once it starts, there's no stopping."

Appa: "Karthik used to be thin."

August 12th, 2018

*Amma's upset I got my eyebrows waxed.*

Amma: "You can't change everything just to fit in. Just think, you can't change the color of your skin."

Me: ??

Amma to Anand: "At least you won't do all this kind of useless stuff."

July 26th, 2018

*stays at home alone*

Amma: "Why don't you have more friends??? You need to be more social."

*hangs out with a friend*

Amma: "Who is she??? ... You can only have one friend that is a girl. And that's your wife. She can be like me: nice and indian."

July 9th, 2018

*Amma's talking to Karthik about the Thai Cave Rescue*

Amma: "Kanna, now don't you go into any caves. You'll get stuck on the way in!"

July 9th, 2018

Appa to Karthik: "You got to choose your college. We should get some say in who you marry."

* Later in the conversation.. *

Amma to Appa: "What did you choose: your college or your wife?"

Appa: "Neither."

June 17th, 2018

Amma on Karthik: "Drinking may have been on the side, but his major bad vice in college was eating."

June 15th, 2018

Amma: "You may want to invest in a weighing machine."

June 7th, 2018

Amma: "Actually, when you look at Karthik, *sometimes*, he doesn't look fat!"

May 11th, 2018

Ammma: "You forget how stupid Karthik is until he comes back."

April 28th, 2018

Me, exasperated: "Jesus Christ!"

Amma: "How come you always say 'Jesus Christ.' Why don't you ever say 'Ganesh Murugah' ??"

April 9th, 2018

Ammma: "So now you've gotten into college, you're just gonna sit back and relax??"

Me: "Yes?"

Amma: "The people who are truly dedicated will start preparing for college immediately."
